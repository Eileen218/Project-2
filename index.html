Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.Frequently in Ghana,children are given their first names as a 'day name'which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: kwasi
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: kwabena
  • Wednesday: kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: kofi
  • Saturday: kwame

    • Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
